Do you have a closet full of clothes, but can't find a thing to wear?

You find a beautiful clothing item, but wonder if it matches anything in your closet?


Then Virtual Closet is for you.

With Virtual Closet (VC) you can identify and label your clothing items so you can:

  • Create your personal profile
  • Add clothes (upload or take a picture)
  • Look at clothes (click for enlarged view)
  • Create outfits for work, weddings, gym. etc.
  • View outfits
  • Delete clothes or outfits
